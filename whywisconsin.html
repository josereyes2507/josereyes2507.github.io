Why Wisconsin

At first, Wisconsin was not where I wanted to go. I wanted to attend University of Michigan in Ann Arbor...oops. At the end of Junior year when I visited Wisconsin I fell in love with it. The whole student scene and social aspect was way better than Michigan's. In addition, Wisconsin offered a great direct study for Economics and provided a great computer science program, which at the time was what I wanted to study. It was also two hours away from home which is far enough where I would be away from home, but close enough to where if I needed to come back, I could.
